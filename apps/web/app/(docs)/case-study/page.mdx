## Case Study: CircleOne Fantasy Disc Golf

**Bringing real-world chaos into perfect sync with automated scheduling**


<img
  src="https://www.circleone.app/api/og"

  className="w-full"

Fantasy Disc Golf runs weekly contests synced to live disc golf events, player availability, and weather conditions. Before, managing the contest schedule required constant developer oversight, manual adjustments, and last-minute patches.

Now, with this scheduling platform:

- **Natural language rules** define contest logic like:  
  _“Create lineup deadlines 1 hour before tee time, only if the weather looks good and all player data is posted.”_

- **Endpoints integrated**:
  - `POST /api/check-players` — to verify player data is live  
  - `GET /api/check-weather` — to ensure conditions are playable  
  - `POST /api/schedule-contest` — to lock in the contest once ready

- **Real-time adjustments**  
  The AI agent checks multiple factors daily. If weather looks bad or data is missing, it reschedules automatically — no human needed.

- **Contextual awareness**  
  A POST to `/api/job/:id` like _“forecast shows storm delay”_ updates the agent instantly, allowing it to defer scheduling without breaking a sweat.

> _“This replaced a patchwork of scripts and Slack alerts. I haven’t thought about ‘when to run things’ in weeks. It just works.”_  
> — Lead Dev, CircleOne

🎯 **Outcome**:  
Dynamic, accurate scheduling with zero manual babysitting. New contests launch on time, even as real-world conditions change.
